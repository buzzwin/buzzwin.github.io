Welcome to Computer Science at UT Austin!

Hey there! Welcome to the exciting world of computer science at UT Austin. Here are some great resources to help you get involved and encourage conversations with other passionate professionals and students:

  • Hacker News: Stay up-to-date with tech news and trends.
  • GitHub: A great platform for collaborative coding and sharing projects.
  • Stack Overflow: Get help from other passionate devs and build your problem-solving skills.
  • UT Austin CS Discord: Connect with peers, ask questions, and join study groups.
  • UT Austin Subreddit: Stay updated on campus events and connect with other students.
  • freeCodeCamp: Free coding tutorials and practice projects.
  • Coursera: Online courses with content from top universities.
